Baghlava

Integredients

  • 1 package of phyllo dough (about 16 oz / 450g)
  • 2 cups of walnuts or pistachios (chopped)
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A few drops of lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Prehear the oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Grease a baking dish(9x13 inches)with butter
  3. Layer 8 sheets of phyllo dough in the dish, brushing each sheet with melted butter.
  4. Mix chopped nuts and cinnamon in a bowl.
  5. Add a thin layer of the nut mixture over the phyllo.
  6. Repeat layering: Add 2 sheets of phyllo, brush with butter, then add nut mixture continue until nuts are used up.
  7. Top with 8 more sheets of phyllo, brushing each with butter.
  8. Cut the baklava into diamonds or squares with a sharp knife.
  9. Bake for 45-50 minutes, until golden and crisp.

    10. For the syrup:

  10. While baking, combine sugar, water, honey, lemon juice, and vanilla in a saucepan.
  11. Boil for about 10 minutes, then let it cool slightly.
  12. When baklava is done, pour the warm syrup over the hot baklava.
  13. Let it soak for a few hours (or overnight) before serving.

Chocolate Cake

Integredients

  • 2 cups purpose flour
  • 1 and ¾ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon solt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water

Instractions

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C
  2. In a large bowl,mix together the flour,sugar,cocoa powder,baking powder,baking soda,and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat well until the batter is smooth.
  4. Gradually add the hot water while mixing. The batter will be a bit thin—don’t worry!
  5. Grease your cake pan and pour the batter into it.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  7. Let it cool, then top with melted chocolate, ganache, or whipped cream if desired.

Macaroni

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta (macaroni)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 250g ground beef or chicken
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped) or ½ cup tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • Some water
  • Optional: bell pepper, mushrooms, or dried herbs

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot, add some salt and oil, then cook the pasta until soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. In another pot, heat the oil and fry the onion until golden.
  3. Add garlic and sauté briefly.
  4. Add ground meat and fry until its color changes.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes or tomato paste, then mix with salt, pepper, and turmeric.
  6. Let the sauce cook with a bit of water so the flavors blend.
  7. Add the drained pasta to the sauce and stir well.
  8. Cover the pot and let it steam on low heat for about 15 minutes.

Grilled Chicken Kebab

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast or thigh (cut into medium cubes)
  • 1 large onion (grated or thinly sliced)
  • 3 tablespoons yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • Brewed saffron or natural food color (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, onion, lemon juice, oil, turmeric, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Add chicken pieces and mix well to coat them with the marinade.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 3 to 5 hours (or overnight) to marinate well.
  4. After marination, skewer the chicken pieces.
  5. Preheat a grill or barbecue, then place the skewers over low heat.
  6. Turn the skewers every few minutes so the chicken cooks evenly and turns golden.
  7. Optionally, brush with melted butter or saffron at the end for extra flavor and tenderness.

Kabuli Pulao

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice
  • 500g lamb or beef (with or without bone)
  • 2 medium onions (chopped)
  • 2 large carrots (julienned thinly)
  • ½ cup raisins (black or golden)
  • ½ tsp saffron or cardamom (optional)
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp cumin (optional)
  • 5 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and soak it in warm water for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and sauté the onions until golden.
  3. Add meat and fry until it changes color.
  4. Add salt, pepper, cinnamon, and cardamom, and stir well.
  5. Add water, cover the pot, and let the meat cook until tender (about 1.5 to 2 hours).
  6. Meanwhile, fry the carrots in a little oil until slightly soft, and sauté raisins separately until they puff.
  7. Once the meat is cooked, strain and keep the broth (to use later for steaming).
  8. In a separate pot, bring water to boil and cook the rice until it's half-cooked. Then drain it.
  9. In the final steaming pot, layer rice, meat, then top with carrots and raisins.
  10. Pour some of the meat broth over the top, cover, and steam on low heat for 30–45 minutes.
